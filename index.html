Welcome to Flight Club 502

About Us

Flight Cub 502 is a non-profit 501(c)(3), based out of Bowman Field (KLOU), Louisville, KY and is dedicated to teaching young students about leadership and success through realistic goal setting in aviation and through Next Gen/STEM education. The organization was established in 2015 by a small group of high schoolers who wanted to inspire other students to become successful in life while using and incorporating aviation and the STEM subjects as a springboard for that success. Flight Club 502 is owned and operated by the youth members with professional adult supervision.
